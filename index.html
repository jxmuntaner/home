Hi! My name is Joseph Xavier.

I am a Senior Economic Adviser at Banca March in Barcelona, where I’m simultaneously finishing a master’s to access the legal profession.

Prior to moving back to Barcelona, I completed my PhD in Financial Economics at the University of Yale.

I did my undergraduate studies at Sciences Po Paris and the University of California, Berkeley and my master’s degree at the London Business School.

I've worked as an international tax consultant, financial adviser and a health economics expert at multinational companies. I’ve also provided expert advice for several government agencies, the IMF and the OECD.

My research interests are in antitrust economics, particularly in the context of the digital economy, economic analysis of international and banking regulation as well as white-collar crime legislation and enforcement. I also have a keen interest in software development, cybersecurity and artificial intelligence.
